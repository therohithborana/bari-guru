Frequently Asked Questions

How does BariGuru work?

BariGuru connects you with verified student writers from various colleges. Simply browse through our writer profiles, choose a writer based on their college and expertise, and contact them directly to discuss your assignment.

How are writers verified?

All writers must submit their student ID cards for verification. We manually verify each application to ensure the authenticity of our writers.

What are the payment terms?

Writers set their own rates per 10 pages. Payment terms should be discussed and agreed upon directly with the writer before starting the assignment.

How do I become a writer?

To become a writer, click on "Apply as Writer", fill out the application form, and submit your student ID for verification. We'll review your application and get back to you within 48 hours.
